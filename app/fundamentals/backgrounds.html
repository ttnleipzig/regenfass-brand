Wallpaper Backgrounds

Regenfass backgrounds now use a repeating Tabler raindrop wallpaper pattern. The goal is simple: make brand surfaces feel recognisable at a glance without stealing focus from portraits, logos, or copy.

These assets are designed for cover-style usage in avatars, hero sections, social graphics, and branded UI surfaces. Every variant keeps the same repeating pattern system, while the base color shifts to one of the main brand colors.

Official guidance lives in `guidelines/BACKGROUND_USAGE.md` .

Usage Principles

Keep the pattern recognisable, but let the content stay in charge.

Do

  • Use one wallpaper color per asset or section.
  • Place logos and text on top with enough breathing room.
  • Prefer the dark blue wallpaper for default branded surfaces.
  • Export raster assets from the official SVG source files.

Avoid

  • Stretching the wallpaper non-proportionally.
  • Stacking multiple wallpaper colors in one small component.
  • Putting dense body copy directly on the texture.
  • Adding extra drop shadows, bevels, or random recolors.

Recommended Applications

These wallpaper assets are made for recurring brand surfaces, not for one-off decorative chaos.
